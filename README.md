# UK-Gender-Pay-Gap-Dashboard-
👩‍🦰👨‍🦰Gender Pay Gap Analytics: UK Gender Pay Gap Dashboard:
A data visualization tool to explore companies data focusing on employees pay for male and women in UK to understand the best metric for a company's pay gap, the average pay gap across the UK and see companies with particularly large pay gaps in favor of men or women.

The Gender Pay Gap Analytics is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to hep uders explore and compare over 10.17 thousand companies across UK. The dashboard focuses on highlighting major companies of different sectors hospitality, construction, business support services and education. This tool is intended for use by UK government department to monitor the gender pay gap across different industries.

The dashboard was built using the following tools:
- 📊Power BI Desktop - Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
- 📂 SQL - Programming language to explore the database.
- 📁 File Format - .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

  Source: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/equality-data-and-gender-pay-gap-reports-2025/gender-pay-gap-report-2025

  Data on  ~1000 employees around London and outside London, including details on their employer name, The average and median percentage differences between the hourly pay of men and women, bonus percent, percentage pay of year quarters and deadline.

  - The UK goverment department was seeing inconsistent pay between male and female gap across companies and could not pinpoint the cause. They needed clarity to demonstrates progress in reducing pay disparities between men and women, and which industries were driving the gap.
  - The goal of this dashbaord to find the average percentage differnces by getting employers name, industry, covered over months, see the companies share of pay difference, identify by area and senior roles to highlight the difference.
